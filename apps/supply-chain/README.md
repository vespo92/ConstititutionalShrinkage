# Supply Chain Management System

## Overview
A revolutionary approach to supply chains that incentivizes regional production through exponential distance-based taxation, reducing environmental impact and increasing resilience.

## Key Principles

### Exponential Distance Taxation
- Tax increases exponentially with supply chain distance
- Formula: `tax = base_tax × e^(distance/threshold)`
- Encourages local production
- Accounts for true environmental cost

### Regional Self-Sufficiency
- Incentivize local manufacturing
- Regional food production
- Distributed energy generation
- Local service provision

### True Cost Accounting
- Environmental impact in pricing
- Carbon footprint calculation
- Water usage tracking
- Social impact assessment

### Smart Supply Networks
- Blockchain-verified supply chains
- Transparent sourcing
- Quality tracking
- Origin certification

## Taxation Model

### Tier System
1. **Hyper-Local** (0-50 miles): 0% additional tax
2. **Regional** (50-200 miles): Base tax rate
3. **Inter-Regional** (200-1000 miles): 2x base rate
4. **National** (1000-3000 miles): 5x base rate
5. **International** (3000+ miles): 10x+ base rate

### Exceptions
- Medical necessities
- Critical technology components
- Emergency supplies
- Specialty items unavailable regionally
